Fibonacci
Discount & Premium
Zones

How to identify where price is cheap or expensive relative to a recent swing — and time your entries accordingly.

Fibonacci Retracement
Below 50%
Discount Zone
Price is "cheap" relative to the range. In an uptrend, this is where you look for long entries — the market is pulling back into value.
Above 50%
Premium Zone
Price is "expensive" relative to the range. In an uptrend, avoid buying here. In a downtrend, this is where you look for shorts.
Midpoint
50% — Equilibrium
The dividing line between premium and discount. Neither cheap nor expensive — the fair value of the swing range.
Best Entry
OTE — 61.8% to 79%
Optimal Trade Entry. Where institutions are believed to place orders. Best risk-to-reward within the discount zone.
